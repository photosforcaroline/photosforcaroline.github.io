before you left i laid up at night wondering how we would keep loving each other the same with 5,730 miles between us -- how i would remember what it felt like to fall asleep beside you, or the smell of your hair after you took a shower. but i realised, no more than 3 or 4 hours after your plane took off for la, that i didn't need any of those things to remember you, or to love you. every day i find you intrinsicly woven into the things that i do and the places that i go, and i wanted to document each of those daily occurences -- for you to know that you're on my mind but also for me, as a reminder that i still love you and will always love you no matter how far we find ourselves from one another.

on this website is one picture a day, every day, since the day you left, and a few words explaining why it reminded me of you. i can't promise i will have the time to update the site daily but i can give you my word that i will keep taking a photo a day and that when i have time this website will be updated with all of them, until the day that we are no longer apart.


i love you sweet girl.
