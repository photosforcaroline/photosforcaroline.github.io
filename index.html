in the weeks leading up to your departure i spent many a night wondering how we would keep loving each other the same with 5,430 miles between us -- how i would remember what it felt like to fall asleep beside you, or the familiar smell of your neck as i hugged you for the first time after just one night apart. but i realised, no more than a few hours after your plane took off for la, that i didn't need any of those things to remember you, or to love you. every day i find you intrinsicly woven into the things that i do and the places that i go, and i wanted to document each of these occurences -- for you to know that you're on my mind but also for me, as a reminder that i still love you and will always love you no matter how far we find ourselves from one another.

on this website is one picture a day, every day, since the day you left, and a few words explaining why each reminds me of you. i can't promise i will have time to update the site daily but i give you my word that i will keep taking a photo a day and that i will post them here at every chance i get, until the day that we are no longer apart.


i love you sweet girl.
